01 / Typography

Inter Variable, dialed in.

Linear uses Inter Variable for everything except code, where Berkeley Mono is the house monospace. Weights are non-standard (510 medium, 590 semibold, 680 bold). Tracking is negative on every size above 12px to keep the surface tight.

02 / Colors & Surfaces

Near-black, never black.

Linear's dark theme is built on a tight stack of cool greys (#08090a → #191a1b) with translucent overlays for elevated surfaces. The brand accent is indigo (#5e6ad2), with a brighter #7170ff for hovers and links.

12-column grid, generous gutters.
